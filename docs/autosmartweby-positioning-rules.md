# Autosmartweby positioning rules

## One-sentence strategy

Autosmartweby creates professional and affordable websites for freelancers, craftsmen and small companies that want to look trustworthy, be easier to find and get enquiries without a complicated marketing or technical project.

## What we sell

We sell:

- trust,
- clarity,
- simple process,
- professional presentation,
- easier contact,
- basic findability,
- peace of mind.

We do not primarily sell:

- AI,
- technology,
- fancy design,
- complicated automation,
- vague marketing transformation.

## Main positioning

Professional website for small businesses without unnecessary stress.

## Supporting positioning

- reliable digital partner,
- digital craftsman,
- verified structure adapted to your business,
- clear price and simple process.

## Homepage must communicate within 10 seconds

- who it is for,
- what the client gets,
- approximate price or price range,
- why they can trust us,
- what the first step is.

## Trust elements

Use concrete trust builders:

- real contact,
- company identity,
- IČO,
- names and people,
- clear process,
- clear ownership,
- clear scope,
- examples,
- references when real,
- FAQ about price, texts, photos, ownership, hosting and edits.

## Anti-patterns

Avoid:

- AI-first messaging,
- fake guarantees,
- fake references,
- vague “modern solution” copy,
- overpromising SEO or leads,
- hidden pricing,
- unclear ownership,
- too much technical language,
- too many animations,
- design that feels too expensive for the target audience.
